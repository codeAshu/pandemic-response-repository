The COVID Tracking Project

https://github.com/COVID19Tracking/covid-tracking-data

Summary

The COVID Tracking Project collects information from 50 US states, the District of Columbia, and 5 other US territories to provide the most comprehensive testing data we can collect for the novel coronavirus, SARS-CoV-2.

Type of product: Data

Platform: Web

Initial audience: USA

Author: The Atlantic, Related Sciences

Link to live product: https://covidtracking.com/

Main point of contact: Twitter @alexismadrigal

Link to community support: https://twitter.com/COVID19Tracking
