Covid-19 patients' self-monitoring at home via forms sent by SMS

https://github.com/lifen-labs/covid

Summary

A patient self-monitoring tool to help healthcare systems monitor COVID patients remotely using text-based follow up. Medical staff can send patients questionaires to monitor their symptoms as they progress or desist and recommend appropriate medical attention.
