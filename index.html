Pandemic Response Repository

A collection of open source digital resources to help governments respond to the Coronavirus.

Project title Description Project tags
COVID-19 Self Assessment Tool The questionnaire takes the public through a series of questions to inform those who are concerned they may have contracted COVID-19. It's based on a modified version of the Alberta Health Services tool to which applied Ontario's design system styling and made a number of functional changes. Self-assessment Health
Tokyo Metropolitan Government COVID-19 Countermeasure Site A data visualization website that conveys an updated, digestible dataset to reflect the status of the spread of coronavirus within the city. The website is open source and can and has been replicated by other interested jurisdictions such as San Francisco. Cases, Testing, Statistics Tracking
Dati COVID-19 Italia Publicly available data on the spread of COVID-19 within Italy. Cases, Maps, Statistics Tracking
2019-nCoV Data Repository by Johns Hopkins CSSE This is the data repository for the 2019 Novel Coronavirus Visual Dashboard operated by the Johns Hopkins University Center for Systems Science and Engineering (JHU CSSE). It is also supported by the ESRI Living Atlas Team and the Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Lab (JHU APL). Cases, Maps, Statistics Tracking
COVID-19 Ask a Scientist Project Users can instantly find answers to COVID-19 questions by clicking the question mark icon at the bottom of the page, or click the "Ask a scientist your question" button to send unanswered questions to a network of scientists and journalists who have volunteered to respond. FAQs, Benefits Information, Assistance
Coronavirus Service Directory A simple directory of services that can help residents cope if they're staying at home due to the coronavirus pandemic. Volunteers, Resource matching Community
SARS-CoV-2 Sequencing Resources This document repository is meant to serve as the start of a crowd-sourced collection of information, documentation, protocols and other resources for public health laboratories intending to sequence SARS-CoV-2 coronavirus samples in the coming weeks. Sequencing, Laboratories Research
nCoV2019 dataset A location for summaries and analyses of data related to n-CoV 2019. Cases, Maps, Demographics Tracking
SARS-CoV-2 Cases communicated by Swiss Cantons and Principality of Liechtenstein A common official OGD dataset of SARS-CoV-2 cases for the Swiss Authorities. SARS-CoV-2 case numbers are provided in machine-readable form (CSV) as OGD resources (Open Government Data), that have been published by official sources (Cantons and FL) online. Sources are specifed with the respective URL(s). Cases, Statistics Tracking
When to call 811 Self-assesment tool to determine if residents of Nova Scotia, Canada should call 811 about COVID-19. Screening, Self-assessment Health
FindTheMasks Rapid response technology to connect personal protective equipment (PPE) donations with healthcare workers in need. Volunteers, Donations, Medical equipment Community
Open Opportunities platform A United States government program that is creating a network of federal employees working together to build a more effective, efficient, responsive government by sharing skills and collaborating on projects that support the mission of an agency or the government as a whole. Collaboration, Resource matching, Crowdsourcing Community
GOV.UK Notify Send emails, text messages and letters to users. Notifications Information
Find COVID Testing A map to make it easy to locate and crowd source COVID-19 testing locations. Maps, Testing, Crowdsourcing Tracking
Splunk Coronavirus Dashboard This project includes an app that allows users to visualize and analyze information about COVID-19 using data made publicly-available by Johns Hopkins University. Cases, Trends, Data visualization Tracking
The COVID Tracking Project This project collects information from 50 US states, the District of Columbia, and 5 other US territories to provide the most comprehensive testing data we can collect for the novel coronavirus, SARS-CoV-2. Cases, Testing, Maps Tracking
Coronavirus in the U.S. - Latest Map and Case Count An ongoing repository of data on coronavirus cases and deaths in the U.S. Cases, Maps Tracking
COVID-19: Novel Coronavirus Infection Map This online interactive map enables users to track both the global and local trends of Novel Coronavirus infection since Jan 21st, 2020. The dataset is timely collected from multiple official sources and then plotted onto this map. Cases, Maps, Trends Tracking
COVID-19 Scenarios This tool explores the dynamics of COVID-19 cases and the associated strain on the healthcare system in the near future. Cases, Modeling, Medical equipment Tracking
California Coronavirus (COVID-19) Response COVID19.CA.gov is designed to answer questions and present actionable and real-time information. It provides a single source of truth for people looking for information about COVID-19 developments and response in California, while providing links back to relevant department websites. FAQs, Symptoms, Testing, Benefits Information, Assistance
Covid-19 patients' self-monitoring at home via forms sent by SMS A patient self-monitoring tool to help healthcare systems monitor COVID patients remotely using text-based follow up. Medical staff can send patients questionaires to monitor their symptoms as they progress or desist and recommend appropriate medical attention. Self-assessment, Monitoring, Symptoms Health
Hamagen React Native Israel's Ministry of Health's COVID-19 Exposure Prevention App. FAQs Health
COVID-19 Hospital Impact Model for Epidemics The CHIME (COVID-19 Hospital Impact Model for Epidemics) Application is designed to assist hospitals and public health officials with understanding hospital capacity needs as they relate to the COVID pandemic. Cases, Statistics, Medical equipment Information
